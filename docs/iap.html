# In-App Purchase Frequently Asked Questions

If you haven't found what you needed, feel free to join the Discord (opens new window) for support or contact us via email at tachimangaapp@gmail.com

# Can the Premium subscription be shared across devices (such as iPhone and iPad)?

Yes, the Premium subscription can be shared across devices.

Please follow these steps:

  1. Install Tachimanga on another device using the SAME Apple ID.
  2. Go to the 'Get Premium' page, click on the Restore Purchase button to enjoy Premium features on the new device.

# What is the renewal policy for the premium version subscription?

The premium subscription will be renewed automatically according to the selected subscription plan (monthly, yearly, etc.) unless you cancel it at least 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period. The payment will be charged to your Apple ID account upon confirmation of the purchase.

# How can I restore my previous purchases?

To restore your previous purchases, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the app and go to the settings -> Get premium.
  2. Look for the "Restore Purchases" option.
  3. Tap on it and enter your Apple ID and password when prompted.
  4. Your previous purchases will be restored, and you will have access to the upgraded premium version.
